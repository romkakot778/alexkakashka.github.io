Hublot and Takashi Murakami are pushing back the boundaries of artistic expression by presenting two NFTs. These are inspired by the Hublot Classic Fusion Takashi Murakami All Black and the Classic Fusion Takashi Murakami Sapphire Rainbow, the first two watches launched jointly by the Swiss watchmaker and the great Japanese artist in 2021. The first NFT, based on the All Black version, is a limited edition of 216, while the second, which will recreate the colours of the highly exclusive Sapphire Rainbow version, will have a supply of 108. These NFTs are mainly intended for the current owners of these two watches, to whom they will be offered under certain conditions.
